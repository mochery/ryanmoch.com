## About

I'm a Principal Platform Engineer working on the architecture and scaling of
mission-critical datacenter infrastructure. My focus is leverage: designing
the platforms and automation other engineers build on, running the technical
governance that keeps cross-functional teams unblocked, and, most recently,
building autonomous AI agent systems that take operational friction out of
the loop entirely.

I've been a full stack developer, an operator, and a support engineer, and
that whole-lifecycle view shapes how I build platforms today: reliability,
cost, and developer velocity are architectural concerns, not afterthoughts.
The day-to-day toolkit is infrastructure and configuration as code:
Terraform, Packer, and Ansible, plus tooling we build in-house.

Away from work I'm usually tinkering: microcontrollers, home servers, 3D
printing, and whatever else has made it onto the bench. The common thread is
curiosity: new tools get a proof of concept in the homelab long before I need
them, and a lot of what I use professionally got its first test run at home.
See [interests]({{ '/interests/' | relative_url }}) for what I'm into, and
[experience]({{ '/experience/' | relative_url }}) for the career details.
